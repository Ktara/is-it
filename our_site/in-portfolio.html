Inmasrter.com.ua

Initiated by our friend Bjorn Blockx and released in August 2015, Ginventory is an app containing more than 3000 differents gins and offering the best serves with approximately 500 different tonic waters and garnishes.

Search for your favourite gin, tonic or garnish and discover the Perfect Serve. Learn all about your best-loved gins and tonic waters. Rate them. Collect them in your personal cabinet or add them on your wishlist. Or buy them directly from selected retail partners.

We built a website inspired by the app: a simple and smooth experience.
